## üêõ Troubleshooting

**Puzzle won't complete?**
- Make sure you're following exact sequence
- Close and reopen puzzle UI to reset

**Can't find tower?**
- Look for glowing lights in the distance
- Check the snow-free areas with tower bases

**Mouse stuck?**
- Press ESC to release cursor
- Click screen to recapture (when not in puzzle UI)

**Performance issues?**
- Lower particle count in scene
- Disable SSAO/SSIL/SSR in environment settings
